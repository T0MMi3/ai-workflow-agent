Support Ticket Routing Policies

1. Billing issues include:
- duplicate charges
- incorrect premium payments
- refund requests
- invoice confusion

Billing issues should usually be marked high priority when they affect payment accuracy
and should be routed to the finance queue.

2. Technical issues include:
- login failures
- application errors
- broken pages
- API failures

Technical issues should usually be marked medium priority
and should be routed to technical support.

3. General issues include:
- basic questions
- account guidance
- informational requests

General issues should usually be marked low priority
and routed to general support.
